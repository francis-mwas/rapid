Who We Are

Get to know us

Rapid analytics And Evaluation is a woman/minority owned consulting firm.Our Consulting Services focus on integrated solutions to federal, State and Local Governments in providing a holistic approach to enhance public health programs. RAEG main focus area includes evaluation and monitoring of public health programs using social science theories and techniques to improve programs and policies while focusing on the customer needs.

RAEG uses synergies of grantee communications and support; data management/data quality assurance, collection and analysis of qualitative and quantitative data, process mapping using the six sigma methodologies among other public health strategies.
