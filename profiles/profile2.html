Meet Godwin Akhirome, MBA, MSc, CPM, FNIM, FIPM, Chief Operating Officer.

Godwin is an astute management executive with experience encompassing financial, government procurement, global procurement and supply chain management. He is a self-starter in many challenging responsibilities within the public and private sectors in the United States and International Corporations.

Before joining Rapid Analytics & Evaluation Group, Godwin, served as the Director of Procurement at the State of Georgia government under various departments . While at the state of GA, he pioneered the leadership of autonomous procurement, contracts and supply chain divisions in the two largest agencies.

He also collaborated with some federal agencies including HHS, CDC, CMS, Social Security Administration and others in the establishment of programs and procurement of services. He retired from the State Government in 2017 and has been working as procurement consultant for a medical devices company and organization and also serving as the COO Rapid Analytics.

In his previous career, Godwin led the establishment of budget and corporate planning division at Total Nigeria, Ltd where he conceptualized and implemented an aggressive market-growth concept that moved the company from the bottom of the seven major petroleum marketing companies in Nigeria to become the market leader within two years.

Godwin is a lead in all operations at Rapid Analytics including serving as the technical advisor on contracts, providing advise throughout the key operations of the organizations.
