Meet Edmund Gumiziriza, MBA Global Public Health Programs.

Mr. Gumisiriza has Over 28 years of experience in public health programs in the United States, Sub-Saharan Africa and the Caribbean region where he has provided technical expertise in the field of HIV prevention programs.

He supported the Division of Global HIV and Tuberculosis of the Center for Global Health within CDC where he provided training, needs assessments and technical assistance to hard to reach populations. He has collaborated with various Ministries of Health including Angola, Botswana, Ethiopia, Kenya, Malawi, South Africa, Trinidad and Tobago, Uganda, and Zimbabwe.

is career evolved into management focusing in strategic planning and proposal writing, implementation, monitoring and quality assurance processes. He is a competent budget analyst. Knowledgeable in training material development and training. Skillful in collaborating and partnering with multiple stakeholders to design, monitor and evaluate programs.

Cognizant of diverse work environments, analyzing situational challenges, and working with teams to develop and implement action plans. Resourceful, intuitive and highly motivated.

In his new role at Rapid Analytics & Evaluation Group Edmund serves as a technical lead for global programs focusing on initiatives to assist developing countries in scaling up their HIV/AIDS prevention programs including Voluntary Testing and Counseling, HIV provider trainings, treatment, care and support.

Edmund earned his Master of Business Administration (MBA) , with a focus in management.
