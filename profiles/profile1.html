Meet Cecily Mbaka, MIS, MPH President and CEO.

With over 20 years of dynamic work experience in public health and business management, Cecily has the unique understanding to execute a systematic synthesis of public health research findings to find strategies that strengthen organizations through translation of evidence into operations and practice.

She has the reputation of getting things done. Ms. Cecily has worked with federal, state and bilateral governments as well as global partners, NGOs and for profit corporations. In these sectors, she has led teams to successful implementation of projects. She has provided technical expertise to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention in the Division of HIV/AIDS, the Center of Global Health and the Global AIDS Program.

She has also worked for the German government under German International Corporation and was the first Administrator of the German Development Bank (KfW) in Kenya. In the recent past, Ms. Cecily, developed an Enhanced Technical Assistance process, a data calibration method to identify data and system issues within a public health government program and developed possible solutions to bridge the gaps and build data quality capacity.

In her position as director of operations for Kurz Inc., Ms. Cecily developed processes that led to increased profits within six months of implementation. Cecily is an expert in program evaluation, qualitative and quantitative data analysis with hands on experience in management, program implementation, system and strategy level evaluations.

She also specializes in the six sigma process mapping, logic model development and data quality technical assistance to the States with a focus area in HIV prevention programs. Ms. Cecily leads Rapid Analytics programs using a holistic approach to enhance public health programs through data translation and evidence-based practice.

Ms. Cecily degrees in Computer Management Information Systems and a Masters degree in Public Health.
